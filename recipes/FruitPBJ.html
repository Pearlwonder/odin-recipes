Peanut Butter & Jelly Smoothie Recipe

Description

This one is for all my workout gurus who are trying to get them gym gains.

Ingredients

  • 6 frozen strawberries (You can add any fruit of preference.)
  • 1 Banana
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of Peanut Butter
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of Grape Jelly
  • 1 tablespoon blue agave nectar
  • 1 tablespoon flax seeds

Steps

  1. Combine all Ingredients inside a blender for 45-60 seconds.
