Mac N Cheese Recipe

Description

This is a step by step recipe on making the classic dish of Macaroni & Cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package whole wheat macaroni (such as Smart Taste)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 21/2 tablespoons all-purpose flower
  • 2 cups shredded low-fat Cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups low-fat (1%) milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup whole wheat bread crumbs
  • 1 pinch paprika

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to boil. Cook elbow macaroni in boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes. Drain.
  2. Melt 2 tablespoons of butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir in flour to make a roux. Slowly add milk to roux, stirring constantly. Stir in Cheddar and Parmesan cheese and cook over low heat until cheese is melted and sauce is thick, about 3 minutes. Place macaroni in large baking dish and pour sauce over macaroni. Stir well.
  3. Melt 2 tablespoons of butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add breadcrumbs and stir until butter is absorbed, 2 to 3 minutes. Spread over macaroni to cover Sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake in preheated oven until cheese sauce is hot and breadcrumbs are browned, about 30 minutes.
